## ğŸ§  Insights Deep Dive

### **Category 1: Speed of Impact**

* **Main insight 1:** Accidents involving **high-speed impacts** recorded the **highest survival rate (53.25%)**, compared to medium-speed (51.25%) and low-speed (45%) impacts.  
* **Main insight 2:** **Speed alone was not the main determinant** of survival; protective gear usage played a key role.  
* **Main insight 3:** Victims in lower-speed impacts often **neglected safety measures**, contributing to lower survival rates.  
* **Main insight 4:** **Safety equipment has a stronger influence** on survival than speed alone.

### **Category 2: Safety Gear (Helmet + Seatbelt)**

* **Main insight 1:** Victims wearing **both a helmet and seatbelt** had the **highest survival rate** of all categories.  
* **Main insight 2:** Those who wore only one type of gear had **moderate survival rates**, while those without any gear had the **lowest**.  
* **Main insight 3:** **Combined use of safety gear** offers the best protection.  
* **Main insight 4:** Lack of compliance correlated with **higher fatality rates**, emphasizing the need for public safety awareness.
